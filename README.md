### The procedure:

Each day we will measure and calculate the sum of the average daily temperature, the incubations and the incubation period by average temperature. Those will provide us with new information every day on when the incubation period will end. May 25 was not taken into consideration as the temperature was below 12Â°C that day.

### Conclusion:

The prognosis, as days go by, continues to suggest that the incubation period will end on May 28 and that the conidium will appear during the night between May 28 and May 29 if the requiring conditions are met - T>12Â°C and RH>85%.
In my country, Serbia, the protection is usually done after the second incubation period as the infections after the first incubation period are rare due to bad conditions for the formation of conidium.
